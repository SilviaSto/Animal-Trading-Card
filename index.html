Orangutan

Orangutans are the largest arboreal mammal. These great apes share 96.4% of our genes and are highly intelligent creatures.

  • Genus: Pongo
  • Habitat: Rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years
  • Status: Critically Endangered

Orangutans are the most solitary of the great apes. They spend most of their time in trees. Fruits are the most important components of an orangutan's diet. Their hair is reddish-brown.
